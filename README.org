** Set-Up
 *Reverie is now available in Obsidian Community Themes.*

  In obsidian go to Settings > Appearance > Community Themes > Browse

 Search for: Reverie by Santi Younger

 Click "Use"

 If you get a window click "Overwrite"

 Done, enjoy!
